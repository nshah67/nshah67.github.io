I am a research associate at Johns Hopkins Bloomberg School of Publis Health working on the Kilkari Impact EValuation. Kilkari is a national audio messaging program that has been rolled out in 12 states of India. As part of this impact evaluation, we are conducted a randomized controll trial enrolling 5000 pregnant women and examing the impact of the content of the messages on knowledge and practice of certain maternal health related behaviors.
